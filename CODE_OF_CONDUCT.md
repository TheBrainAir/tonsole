# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the `tonsole` project a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful, welcoming, and considerate.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Trolling, or other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and when an
individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers
through a private GitHub Security Advisory or the contact listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
Maintainers will review and investigate all complaints and respond in a way they deem appropriate.
Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this Code of
Conduct.
